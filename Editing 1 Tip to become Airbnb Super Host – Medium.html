One Easy Tip to Be Airbnb Super Host

Airbnb has been increasingly popular these days. If you have extra space, Airbnb can be a great extra source of income. And if you do well and are among the top 20%, you might become a super host and get a badge on your profile.

You might ask what benefit it could bring. Well, you might get extra exposure and revenue. On Airbnb, customers are invited to review their experience. In reviews of super hosts, ‘highly recommended’ is mentioned much more frequently (Figure 1 and 2).

Then you may ask, how do I become a super host? Certainly, it takes time (usually one to two years of record on Airbnb) and some common sense procedure (like verifying yourself on the platform). But one thing that people tend to ignore but easy to do is managing your listing description.
